About Our Project

Goal

Our project aims to create, integrative and easy to use smart house system, based on the Internet of things. With other modern technologies we used some AI features, to make day to day activities even easier to manage.

What We Offer

Our automated system gives user ability to manage all the home devices from one place and remotely control home functions. With our application user can control light and door lock systems and much more from anywhere.

Safety First

Our project is not only focused on the comfort but also safety of the costumer. We are maximizing home security by using alerts for the break of the window and by detecting life and house damaging factors such a fire, CO, etc

What Are We Proud Of

This smart house system creates the friendly environment for people with various disabilities by making different house tasks more manageable
